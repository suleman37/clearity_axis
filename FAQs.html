Frequently Asked Questions

What is an e-course?

+

An e-course is an online course that allows you to learn at your own pace through video lectures, readings, quizzes, and interactive content.

How do I enroll in a course?

+

To enroll, simply browse our course catalog, select the course you’re interested in, and click the "Enroll" button. You will need to create an account if you don’t have one.

What are the payment options?

+

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, Pynoer, and other online payment systems. Please check our payment page for more details.

Is there a free trial available?

+

Yes, we offer a free trial for many of our courses. You can explore the course content and see if it meets your needs before committing.

What types of courses do you offer?

+

We offer a wide range of courses across various subjects, including technology, business, arts, health, and personal development. You can browse our catalog for specific topics.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

+

Yes, upon successfully completing a course, you will receive a certificate that you can download and share.

What are the technical requirements for taking a course?

+

You will need a computer or mobile device with internet access. Some courses may require specific software or applications, which will be listed in the course description.

Is there a community or forum for students?

+

Yes, we have a community forum where students can interact, ask questions, and share insights. You can find it in your course dashboard.

How can I contact customer support?

+

You can reach our customer support team via email, live chat, or through the contact form on our website. We aim to respond within 24 hours.

What is your refund policy?

+

We offer a 07-day money-back guarantee for most courses. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a refund within 07 days of enrollment.

Can I change my course after enrolling?

+

Yes, you can switch to a different course within the first 7 days of enrollment. Please contact our support team for assistance.

Do you offer group discounts?

+

Yes, we provide discounts for groups or organizations. Please contact us for more information on bulk enrollment.
